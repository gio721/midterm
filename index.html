Montefiore Square Reimagined

A youth-centered redesign transforming Harlem’s public plaza
into a vibrant, social, and interactive hub.

About the Space

Montefiore Square, located in Harlem, New York, sits at a major intersection with strong pedestrian flow. Despite its recent redesign, the space remains underutilized—especially by teens and young people.

This project proposes a transformation focused on interaction, safety, culture, and community engagement.

Our Vision

  • AI Chatbot Hub: Interactive kiosk for surveys, directions, and event discovery.
  • Performance Space: Open mic stage for music, poetry, and youth events.
  • Social Seating: Comfortable areas for studying, relaxing, and gathering.
  • Night Lighting: LED lighting to improve safety and nighttime energy.
  • Pop-Up Market: Rotating vendors, food stands, and student-run businesses.

Why This Matters

Public spaces shape how communities interact. By designing Montefiore Square for youth engagement, we create a safer, more active, and culturally rich environment.

Increased foot traffic leads to stronger local businesses, better safety, and a stronger sense of community identity.

AI Integration

An AI-powered chatbot (built using n8n workflows) will allow visitors to:

  • Submit real-time feedback
  • Vote on future events
  • Discover activities happening in the plaza

This ensures the space evolves based on the community’s needs.
