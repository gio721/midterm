Montefiore Square Reimagined

A youth-centered redesign transforming Harlem’s public plaza
into a vibrant, social, and interactive hub.

About the Space

Montefiore Square sits at a major intersection in Harlem with strong pedestrian flow. Despite recent improvements, the space remains underutilized—especially by teens and young people.

This project reimagines the plaza as a dynamic, youth-focused environment built around interaction, culture, and safety.

Our Vision

  • 🤖 AI Chatbot Hub – Interactive info + surveys
  • 🎤 Performance Space – Open mic + live events
  • 🪑 Social Seating – Study & hangout areas
  • 🌃 Night Lighting – Safe + vibrant evenings
  • 🛍️ Pop-Up Market – Food + local vendors

Why This Matters

Public spaces shape how communities interact. By designing Montefiore Square for youth engagement, we create a safer, more active, and culturally rich environment.

Increased foot traffic leads to stronger businesses, better safety, and a stronger sense of community identity.

AI Integration

An AI-powered chatbot (built using n8n workflows) allows visitors to:

  • Submit real-time feedback
  • Vote on events
  • Discover activities in the plaza
