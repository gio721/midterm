Revitalize Montefiore Square

Transforming Harlem’s public space into a pedestrian-friendly, vibrant community hub

About Montefiore Square

Montefiore Square is a small public park located in Harlem, NY. Currently underutilized, the square has the potential to become a hub for pedestrian activity, community gatherings, and local cultural events.

Project Goals

  • Increase pedestrian traffic and comfort
  • Enhance safety and accessibility
  • Encourage local business and cultural activity
  • Integrate AI systems for community feedback

Current Challenges

  • Lack of seating and shaded areas
  • Scattered pedestrian flow and underused pathways
  • No organized events or community engagement
  • Limited visibility for local businesses

Before & After: Montefiore Square Redesign

Before: Open hexagonal pavement, sparse benches, minimal pedestrian activity.

After: Pedestrian-friendly redesign with seating, greenery, event spaces, and murals.

Benefits & Impact

  • Higher pedestrian traffic and more vibrant public life
  • Support for local businesses and cultural initiatives
  • Enhanced community engagement and inclusion
  • Data-driven improvements via AI feedback
