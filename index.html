Montefiore Square: Current Challenge

Montefiore Square has strong pedestrian flow but lacks youth engagement, interactive elements, and vibrant community spaces. Many teens, students, and residents pass through without meaningful experiences.

Our Vision

  • 🤖 AI Chatbot Hub for information and surveys
  • 🎤 Performance Space for live music and events
  • 🪑 Social seating and recreational areas
  • 🌃 Night lighting to improve safety and aesthetics
  • 🛍️ Pop-up markets for food & crafts

Design Proposal

The proposed redesign transforms Montefiore Square into a pedestrian-friendly, youth-centered plaza. It encourages social interaction, creativity, and community engagement through flexible spaces, seating, and event areas.
