Montefiore Square

A vision to transform the 138th & Broadway corridor into
West Harlem's premier pedestrian commons.

By prioritizing community commerce and student safety, we propose converting underutilized granite terraces into active hubs. Our plan features permanent open-air markets, solar-powered study kiosks for CCNY students, and high-visibility pedestrian safety improvements.

  • Safety: Vision Zero Lighting
  • Culture: Harlem Heritage Murals
  • Connectivity: Smart Wayfinding
